About

I've studied social sciences at Kyiv-Mohyla Academy—a unique and old university in Kyiv, Ukraine—and group psychoanalysis afterwards. And yet, it was English that I've chosen to work with, so I joined the tech field as a content writer / marketing copywriter.

Currently, I'm learning to mark up, style, and code websites and web apps. I'm also a proud student at FrauenLoop (the first cohort of this decade!) and DCI Berlin.

In my spare time, I translate and subtitle films, enjoy city walks, hike in the nature, or pick mushrooms. Mountains and now coding are my most favorite things in the world.
